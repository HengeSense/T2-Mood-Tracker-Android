Main Screen

The main screen allows you to perform several functions. You can Add a Note by touching the icon on the top right corner of the screen.

You can also navigate to any of the Rating Categories which include Anxiety, Depression, General Well-Being, Head Injury, Post-traumatic Stress, or Stress.

The Results area takes you to graph results which display all of your previous ratings, and allows you to view notes you have previously entered.

The lower area of the screen provides additional information about T2 Mood Tracker, provides you with the opportunity to provide written feedback about the app directly to the developers, offers this help information, and allows you to rate the app.

The Settings area contains a Category Editor which allows you to hide any of the rating categories that may not interest to you.

You can also set a reminder so you don’t forget to make your ratings. At any point you can clear all of your ratings from any of the categories and clear your notes as well. You can also enable security using a PIN and enable or disable the health tips on startup.

Knowing how T2 Mood Tracker is being used helps the developers to modify and upgrade the app based on anonymous usage patterns. This information DOES NOT compromise your personal anonymity in any way. You may disable this function if you choose, however. The last function on the list allows you to tell a friend about the app using an email message which includes download information.

Rate Your Mood

By selecting the mood category you’d like to rate you will see a screen with ten opposing pairs of feeling states like happy-sad. To rate how you are doing, move the slider with your finger to the place on the scale that best reflects how you are feeling at the moment.

Make sure you rate yourself on each of the ten pairs of feelings by scrolling to the very bottom of the screen. When you have completed your ratings, touch “save” and you will be returned to the main screen. Your rating will be automatically included on the graph for that mood category.

Reviewing Your Results

By selecting Graphed Results from the main screen you can view all of your ratings by date. To view graphs of previous or subsequent months use the arrows beneath the graph. You can view your results on any combination of mood categories by toggling each one ON or OFF.

Each point on the graph represents the average of your mood ratings for each of the ten opposing pairs of feeling states for that mood category.

You can view your notes by touching Notes. There is a small triangle shaped indicator on the top of the graph indicating which dates have notes associated with them. You can add a note for any date by touching “Add a Note” at the top of the screen.

Upward trends in the graphs represent more positive self-ratings. By touching any of the Mood Category labels(e.g., Anxiety), you can display the graphs of your self-ratings for each of the ten pair of opposing feeling states. As with the category graphs, you can display or hide each of the graphs by toggling them ON and OFF.

You can clear your graphs by going to Settings > Clear Result Data

Managing Notes

By touching View/Edit Notes on the Main Screen, you can see all of your notes with the most recent note you’ve made on top. Any note that has more text than is displayed has an indicator alerting you that there is additional text. By touching the note, the full text is displayed and the keyboard is made available to edit the note if desired. You can also create a new note from this screen. You can clear your notes by going to Settings > Clear Result

Creating Custom Categories

You can create a custom self-rating category by going to Settings > Groups on the main screen. Touch the + in the upper right hand corner of the screen. You can name your new category and then touch Manage Scales. You will be able to name to name opposing descriptors for that category. The left label should represent the “negative” feeling and the right label, the “positive” feeling. You can make as many custom categories as you like.
